### Special stable tracks

- Railway schools: prioritize former铁道部 lineage and铁路局定向/合作渠道.
- Power schools: prioritize local state-grid/southern-grid feeder colleges.
- Police/司法警校: if符合政审、体测、体检 and has联考/便捷入警 path, employment certainty can be much stronger than ordinary专科.

## Answering Rules

- For本科 candidates: tie each school to a strong major. A school is not automatically "good" outside its specialty.
- For高职/专科 candidates: foreground employable skills,实训条件,订单班, local industry, and whether the student accepts technical work.
- Avoid prestige-only advice for 300-500 分. Ask whether the user values升本/就业/入编/铁路电力/本地发展.
- Always warn that vocational school names have changed quickly in recent years; verify the current official name and招生层次.
