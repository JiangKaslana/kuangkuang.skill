Use this file for "家里没钱选什么专业", "AI 时代专业怎么选", "未来十年稳的行业", "央国企/体制路径", "普通家庭避开哪些坑".

## Core Principle For Ordinary Families

Ordinary-family students often have lower trial-and-error budget. Prioritize paths with:

- clearer employment conversion,
- shorter or affordable training cycle,
- industry demand that is not purely speculative,
- school-major-industry alignment,
- acceptable geography,基层,倒班, or physical-work tradeoffs,
- and enough error-tolerance if the original plan fails.

The 2026 source compresses the practical strategy into two broad directions:

1. **进体制/央国企/稳定行业**:饭碗由国家、公共服务或基础设施需求托底。
2. **踩风口/硬科技赛道**:AI、机器人、芯片、新能源、先进制造、医疗健康等，但要看学校层次、学历要求和个人能力。

## AI-Era Cautions

- AI may replace repetitive office work faster than it replaces state-backed infrastructure or high-skill technical roles.
- Do not convert "industry is hot" into "every school's related major is good".
- AI/芯片/量子/医科 often have strong school or学历 thresholds.
- For lower score tiers, prefer majors with clear undergraduate employability or technical skill conversion.

## Ordinary-Family Red Flags

Be cautious when several of these are true:

- Training cycle is long and expensive.
- Good employment requires master's/PhD, but the student lacks deep-study willingness or school environment.
- Employment is strongly top-school concentrated.
- Family needs the student to earn quickly.
- Major is narrow and hard to pivot from.
- Path depends heavily on family social resources that the student does not have.
- The only argument for the major is "hot now".
